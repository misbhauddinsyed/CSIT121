Recipe: Hyderabadi Biryani

Author: Swathi Recipe

Ingredients

Spicies

  • 2 and 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 slit green chillies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Vegetables

  • Onions
  • Green Chillies
  • Mint leaves
  • Curry leaves
  • Cilantro
  • Lemons

Main Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • 250gm Chicken
  • Pure Ghee
  • Garam Masala
  • Vegetable Oil


How to Prepare Hyderabadi Biryani

  1. Take a mixing bowl and mix all the spicies in it along with 165ml yogurt.
  2. After mixing, Take 250gms of chicken and marinate it with the mix of spicies and keep it for overnight.
  3. Take 1 and 1/2 cup basmati rice and boil it with garam masala spicies.
  4. Take a big frying bowl, add 1/4 cup of vegetable oil and pure ghee along with onions and fry it.
  5. Add the marinated chicken to the fried onions and stir it well for half an hour.
  6. Add the garam masala basmati rice on top of the cooked chicken and leave it for couple of hours with tight lid.
  7. After 2 hours take out the lid, your biryani is ready.
  8. Add some cilantro, squeeze lemon for better flavour.

Ps: I like this recipe because hyderabadi biryani is world famous and comes from my home town Hyderabad and it is very delicious and I recommend every one to try it.
