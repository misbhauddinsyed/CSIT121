Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Averio was born on February 5 1985, in Funchal, Portugal. He was named the richest fotball player in the world with an estimated worth of $450 million. He is the leading goal scorer in international football and the only active footballer with the most number of club goals. Ronaldo started his career from playing for small portugese club called sporting CP and later joined Manchester United.
